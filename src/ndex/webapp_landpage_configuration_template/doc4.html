NDEx, the Network Data Exchange:
A Resource for Network Analysis, Collaboration and Publication
Use the Search Panel above to find public networks in NDEx. Click on "Featured Networks" for examples of high-quality content from multiple sources.
NDEx is for everyday work and collaboration, a "Dropbox for networks" with 10 GB of free storage. Your networks can be private or shared with other NDEx users or groups.
Programs can read, write, and query NDEx via its REST API, using our client libraries in Python, R, and Java and JavaScript components. Integrate NDEx in your workflows as a source, destination, or intermediate storage.
Your publications and grant proposals can go beyond static figures, linking directly to your networks in NDEx as live, actionable data. NDEx is a recommended repository for Scientific Data, Springer Nature, and PLOS.
NDEx is an integral part of Cytoscape: find networks, download networks, and upload networks directly from Cytoscape. Vice versa, networks viewed in the NDEx Web UI can be seamlessly opened in Cytoscape with just one click.
